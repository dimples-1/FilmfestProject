Reserve your seat. Be part of the story.

Join us at Film Festival 2025 and experience a world of cinematic brilliance, live talks, and unforgettable moments. Choose a ticket that suits your experience—from full access passes to single-entry screenings.

🎫 Ticket Categories & Pricing

Ticket Type Inclusions Price
🎥 Full Festival Pass Access to all film screenings, panels, workshops, and Awards Night (3 days) ₱1,500
🎬 Day Pass Access to all screenings & events on your selected day ₱600
🎞️ Single Screening Admission to one film of your choice ₱150
🧑‍🎓 Student Pass Full 3-day access for students with valid ID (limited slots) ₱900
🎤 Workshop Only Pass Entry to all workshops & panel sessions only (no film screenings) ₱500

📍 All passes include access to exhibits and the Filmmakers’ Lounge.

🛒 How to Buy Tickets

Online Booking:

Visit our official booking partner click the “Book Now” button below.

On-Site Purchase:

Limited walk-in tickets available at the venue entrance starting 9:00 AM each day.

Group Bookings & Inquiries:

For school groups or corporate bookings, email us at tickets@filmfest2025.com.

🔔 Important Notes

  • 🎟️ Seats are limited. Reserve early to secure your slot.
  • 🧾 Tickets are non-refundable but transferrable.
  • 📩 You will receive a confirmation email or e-ticket after purchase.
  • 📸 Present a digital or printed ticket at the entrance.
